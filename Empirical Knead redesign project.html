Empirical Knead Website Redesign Case Study

What makes a business unique and successful?

Design a new website for Atlanta local massage business Empirical Knead to attract more clients and increase profit.

My Role

I was responsible for the user research, definition, ideation, wireframe, prototype and user testing. I designed high fidelity wireframe and used Adobe XD for prototype.

The problem

The owner of Empirical Knead wants to crease his hourly rate and get more clients, but his current website, branding, online marketing and web existent doesn’t support or attract new clients to book. How might we improve his brand image overall and create a more high-end feel website and online existence to persuade visitors to book therefore increase Dedric’s hourly rate price.
